My Favorite Samuel L. Jackson Roles

Jules Winnfield, Pulp Fiction

Jules represents the voice of reason in Pulp Fiction. The contrast between him and Vincent Vega depicts the difference between quitting while you're ahead and being too subborn to quit. He also demonstrates the most character growth out of any character in the film, seeing the coincidence of him quoting Ezekiel 25:17 and seeing that has more to do with his own journey than it does with anything else.

Elijah Price, Unbreakable

Elijah is the super villain of the story who helps Bruce Willis's character discover and develope his powers.
